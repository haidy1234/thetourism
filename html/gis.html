El Gouna Film Festival . The 6th Edition of El Gouna Film Festival is announced to be held from October 13-20,2023 The mission of El Gouna Film Festival is to showcase a diverse range of films catering to a creative and well-versed audience. The festival is dedicated to the discovery of new voices and strives to be a catalyst for the development of cinema in the Arab world, particularly through its industry segment, CineGouna Platform. GFF is also pleased to announce that festival Co-Founder and Entrepreneur Amr Mansi, who played a pivotal role in the establishment and success of GFF, will be its new Executive Director.

El Gouna Half Marathon. For the fifth year in a row, The TriFactory will organise El Gouna Half Marathon, the Red Sea’s premier running event that takes place in the heart of Egypt’s most iconic resort town. The running race will take place on Friday, November 11th and will offer runners four distances to choose from: the 21K, 10K, 5K, and the 1K Kids Race for young runners ages 5-10. Every year, various improvements are made to the race course, and this year will be no different. Runners can expect to begin at Abu Tig Marina, after which the route will take them on an exciting tour of El Gouna including Downtown, the Festival Plaza, Abydos Marina, the new sports hub, golf course, and different lagoons, before finishing where they began.
